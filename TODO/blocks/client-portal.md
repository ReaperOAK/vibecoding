# Block: Branded Client Portal

**Block ID:** BLOCK-FE001
**Capability Reference:** TODO-FE001
**Description:** Premium, customizable client-facing portal for deliverables, feedback, and approvals. Supports branding, secure access, and a polished experience for clients.

## Sub-Blocks

1. **FE001-1: Portal Shell & Routing**
   - Set up portal routes, authentication, and layout
   - **Effort:** 0.5 day
   - **Owner:** Frontend

2. **FE001-2: Deliverable Review & Feedback**
   - UI for clients to review deliverables, leave feedback
   - **Effort:** 1 day
   - **Owner:** Frontend

3. **FE001-3: Milestone Approval Workflow**
   - Approve/reject milestones, track status
   - **Effort:** 0.5 day
   - **Owner:** Frontend

4. **FE001-4: Client Branding & Customization**
   - Support per-client branding, logo, and theme
   - **Effort:** 0.5 day
   - **Owner:** Frontend/UIDesigner

5. **FE001-5: Secure Access & Permissions**
   - Enforce RBAC, secure client-only views
   - **Effort:** 0.5 day
   - **Owner:** Backend/Frontend

## Dependencies
- BLOCK-DO001 (cloud infra/CI)
- BLOCK-BE008 (auth/RBAC)
- BLOCK-BE001 (multi-tenant core)
- BLOCK-FE002 (UI design system)
